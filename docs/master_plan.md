# AI-Powered Urban Air Quality Intelligence

## Project Overview

### Problem Statement

Develop an AI-powered system that analyzes urban air quality by combining multiple real-time data sources to identify the most likely causes of high AQI, predict pollution trends, and generate actionable recommendations for government authorities and citizens.

---

## Project Vision

Instead of only displaying the Air Quality Index (AQI), the system should answer:

- Why is AQI high?
- What is causing the pollution?
- How confident is the system?
- What actions should the government take?
- What precautions should citizens follow?

The goal is to transform raw environmental data into intelligent, explainable, and actionable insights.

---

## Objectives

- Collect data from multiple trusted sources.
- Analyze pollution using AI.
- Identify pollution sources.
- Generate confidence scores.
- Recommend actions for authorities.
- Provide health advisories for citizens.
- Visualize everything on an interactive dashboard.
